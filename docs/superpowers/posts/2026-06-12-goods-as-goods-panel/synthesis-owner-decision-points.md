(a) Runtime `Good(u16)` + Vec-backed columns, count from config (verified: the existing per-resource folds carry no count word — hash.rs:399-419, config.rs:607-633 — so 2-good worlds keep byte-identical hash streams and the refactor commit is provably hash-neutral; Experiment C's 10→20 becomes config-only; exactly one state bump per rung). **OD-2 — The goods money counterparty (and the honest solvency arithmetic).**
Goods move for free today and stations hold no money (economy.rs:42-47); without buy/sell legs, corps are a finite ~2000-delivery battery and D6's wage channel dies mid-run. (a) One Exchange corp (the Yard/Port config-index idiom), merged with the Port AND the Yard in scenario_bazaar, covering every station including the haven; battery sized from a calibration run's measured drain; Exchange-drain printed as a standing read. Attached honesty: this is a sized wind-down battery, not a closed loop (worst drain ≈ 5.4e9/100k ticks vs ≤0.3e9 refuel recapture; ransom wallets are a true sink) — late-game depression is a watchable possibility, not a bug. (b) Per-station owner corps: geography-true, feeds posture flavor, ~10× the config surface and the same drain arithmetic. (c) Consumption-minted money (sink recipes pay the Exchange): the only self-sustaining variant, but the first MINT leg in the credit identity. **Recommend (a)**, with (c) as the named trigger if the console shows universal heat death.

**OD-3 — Fuel price re-bake in scenario_bazaar.**
At the frontier's calibrated base[Fuel]=5_000, max fuel gross = 135k micros < any plausible ~200k wage floor — a fuel package can NEVER clear the trigger and WA4's emergent tanker is arithmetically unreachable. (a) Re-bake base[Fuel] ≈ 50_000 in scenario_bazaar only (frontier untouched): fuel arbitrage clears short/mid legs AND a dry-station fill becomes ~2M ≈ one wage — arming rung B's robbed→broke→stranded supply-fragility chain. (b) Oversize fuel package qty: collides with hold capacity and the qty-5 retail floor (scenario.rs:104). (c) Accept WA4 structurally empty and record it. **Recommend (a).**

**OD-4 — The wage formula, which fixes what WB1 may honestly claim.**
(a) Wage = static transport floor + share of spread surplus (`wage_share_milli`): robs sink cargo, starved destinations hold spreads open, dangerous-lane wages rise through pure price mechanics — WB1 stays as registered with zero danger-awareness and zero shaping; time-to-claim (ASSIGN's real evidence penalty) is the secondary read; the read is distance-conditioned and reports posting-rate. (b) Cost-plus wage: lane-invariant by construction; WB1 must be reframed as claim latency (an owner-approved window rewritten). (c) Evidence-priced wage now (`rob_evidence × danger_micros`): direct, but WB1 then merely confirms our own formula, and it pre-empts the emergent read. **Recommend (a).**

**OD-5 — Ratify the omniscience principle (this recurs at INFO-LAYER).**
D2 seals crate contents "to everyone at engagement." (a) Ratify: the lottery-ticket law binds POLICY reads (scripted brains, future learned heads), never the unhashed event stream — events are the owner's omniscient window (CrateSeized carries contract + contents: dramatic irony at the console + the genealogy join key) — WITH two riders: contents unseal on seizure (a pirate may read its own hold for fence-seeking; the engagement decision stays content-blind), and no gossip/media mint may ever source a sealed payload field (the back-door leak guard). (b) Sealed events until Fenced: dramatically pure, makes the genealogy join and several WB reads needlessly indirect. (c) Full provenance store now: a second schema + pre-built fields nothing reads. **Recommend (a).**

**OD-6 — Does the ransom leg fire on own-trader robs?**
(a) Yes — takings are prey-agnostic; a pressed-and-robbed trader loses goods AND wallet, which is precisely the WB4 fuel (a robbed trader below the 6M cap is emptied and cannot refuel; the dry-ship beat can actually fire). (b) No — goods only: softer dark channel, but it leaves the refuel wallet intact and quietly re-kills the beat this rung exists to fire, and it biases the D6 mode mix beyond what the publicity trade-off grants. (c) Reduced dark-channel cap: a tuning middle with a new knob nothing else needs. **Recommend (a)**, revisit only if the console shows own-trade collapsing to nonviable.
