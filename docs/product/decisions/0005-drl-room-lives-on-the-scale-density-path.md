## Context

The vertical-slice shaping pass (`jumpgate-818a04bb6b`; workflow `wf_9a8eecdb-ccd`,
28 agents, adversarial on RAID R5) graded six candidate DRL arenas inside the
demand-driven economy slice by fraction-of-ceiling (can a learner beat the *best
fixed script* by a measurable margin?) and N-agent trainability. Result: **no DRL
room is demonstrable inside the thin tractable economy.** Three candidates are
provably presolvable (closed-form → dead); three are LOW/MEDIUM-ceiling and
unmeasured (predicted sub-gate by precedent). Crucially, *buildability is
anti-correlated with room*: the arenas adjacent to the first loop are the most
presolvable, and the only MEDIUM-ceiling arena (navy vs co-adapting pirates) needs
the apex trophic stack plus two-population co-training the project has never
converged.

This **reconfirms** (does not newly discover) the project's six prior empirical
probes and the consolidated principle `vsl-cannot-host-judgment-principle`:
**replay-determinism IS presolvability**, so a small, replayable, few-actor market
hosts *computation, not judgment* — DRL can only tie the best script there. The
spine of every NO-GO is *self-cancellation* (LLN self-averaging, risk
self-equalization, clamped/closed-form markets). The one prior GO
(`ecosystem-oscillation`) created room via a *coupled feedback loop held off
equilibrium* (food-driven predator-prey → sustained disequilibrium) — but it was
judged by **play, with heuristic agents and zero RL**.

PDR-0002 fixed the venue as strategic/operational inside the demand-driven
ecosystem and noted that venue was net-new. The shaping pass sharpens *where within
it* the DRL thesis can actually be proven: not the thin market.

## Options considered

1. **Build a DRL arena inside the small economy and claim the thesis** — rejected:
   this is the exact graveyard pattern; the thesis would quietly fail (RAID R5
   materialized).
2. **Reposition the DRL thesis to the scale/density/population path** — keep the
   measurable-better-decisions construct, but site it where doc-25 says room lives:
   intractable density, population-scale contention, co-learning (minds you cannot
   enumerate / a system held off its attractor). Build the first loop as a
   deterministic correctness/replay **harness** regardless (it is the substrate the
   dense arena needs), and gate the first DRL bet behind a cheap analytic cut on the
   real substrate. — chosen.
3. **Reframe the done-definition to entertaining-emergence (game-room)** — accept
   measurable-DRL-beats-script is dead in a small market and judge v1 by emergent
   play instead. Rejected as the *primary* frame: its only exemplar used heuristics,
   so "does DRL beat *good heuristics* at being entertaining" is itself unproven and
   possibly as hard — it trades one hard problem for another, not for an easy win.
   (Not foreclosed; revisitable if the scale path stalls.)

## The call

Option 2 (owner, 2026-06-09). The DRL thesis vehicle moves from "inside the thin
market" to the **scale/density/population path**. Concretely:

- The **first economic loop** (producers → stock → contracts → haulers →
  demand-deflation pricing) is built as a **deterministic harness** — charter-locked,
  additive on live seams, low-risk — and is *explicitly not* where the DRL win is
  claimed.
- The **DRL-room bet** is a separate, later design: a dense/population arena on top
  of that substrate, where the optimum is not presolvable.
- Before any learner is trained for the first candidate, run the **cheap analytic
  cut** (constant → best-closed-form → per-seed-myopic → omniscient-DP, on held-out
  seeds, telemetry-ablated, vs best-closed-form never uniform-random). Honor a NO-GO
  if the best-closed-form→omniscient gap < 10% of ceiling.

## Consequences

- The charter done-definition stands (measurable strategic/operational
  DRL-vs-scripted differential) but its *arena* is now the dense/population path,
  not the thin market. Charter land-order updated accordingly.
- A future shaping pass must design the scale/density arena itself (what is dense,
  what is hidden/co-adapting, what is the falsifiable metric). That is the real
  thesis-vehicle design and depends on the harness existing first.
- RAID R5 transforms from an open risk to a *characterized + accepted-mitigation*
  item (the repositioning is the mitigation).

## Reversal trigger

If the scale/density arena, once designed and measured, *also* fails the 10%
fraction-of-ceiling gate (no room at feasible N-agent throughput, R6), then the
measurable-decisions thesis is falsified for v1 and the project should fall back to
Option 3 (reframe done-definition to entertaining-emergence) or re-scope the bet —
not keep moving the arena. One repositioning, not an infinite search.
